# Mission: Agentic AI Architecture

## Why
Build enough practical fluency to explain, compare, and design agentic AI systems using clear architectural vocabulary, especially around agents, environments, tools, planning, memory, monitoring, and control boundaries.

## Success looks like
- Explain what makes a system agentic without reducing it to "an LLM with tools."
- Choose an appropriate agent type and architecture for a concrete workflow.
- Compare planning and reasoning patterns by cost, latency, reliability, and operational risk.
- Sketch a design with explicit perception, reasoning, action, memory, monitoring, approval, and recovery paths.
- Defend when not to use a more complex multi-agent or self-correcting pattern.

## Constraints
- Use the three local agentic AI markdown files as the starting curriculum.
- Keep lessons short, practical, and tied to design decisions.
- Favor retrieval practice and small design exercises over passive reading.

## Out of scope
- Implementing a production agent framework from scratch.
- Deep mathematical treatment of reinforcement learning, MCTS, or formal planning.
- Vendor-specific platform training unless it supports the architecture concepts.
